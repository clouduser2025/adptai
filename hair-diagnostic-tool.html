Hair Diagnostic System

Presenting Adapt AI's “Hair Diagnostic System” – An innovative AI-driven solution for diagnosing hair conditions and providing personalized care recommendations.

Key Features:

  • Detection of hair-related issues (e.g., dandruff, hair loss, scalp conditions)
  • Personalized treatment recommendations based on diagnosis
  • AI-driven analysis of scalp images
  • Progress tracking with periodic scans
  • Integration with dermatological platforms

Ideal Skills and Experience:

  • Expertise in computer vision and image processing
  • Experience with dermatological datasets and analysis
  • Proficiency in deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch
  • Knowledge of hair and scalp biology
  • Familiarity with personalized recommendation systems

Development Process:

  • Step 1: Collect and preprocess a dataset of scalp and hair condition images.
  • Step 2: Train machine learning models for accurate condition detection.
  • Step 3: Implement treatment recommendation algorithms.
  • Step 4: Test the system with diverse user profiles.
  • Step 5: Deploy as a mobile app or integrate with dermatology systems.
